About us

Barefoot Floor & Fabric Care is Matt and Melissa Cooper. We have one goal: to offer our customers the best value on the cleanest floors and furniture on the beach.

If you have carpet, furniture, tile, hardwood, or vinyl that need to be cleaned, please give us a call at 252-548-9030 and we'll stop by to give you a free estimate. We are also available 24/7 for emergency water extraction.

We’ve been in business for over ten years serving all areas of the Outer Banks of North Carolina including but not limited to Corolla, Duck, Kitty Hawk, Kill Devil Hills, Nags Head, Manteo, Wanchese, Rodanthe, Waves, and Salvo.

Our services

We clean all of the following and more!

Carpet

We first pre-treat your carpet’s visible spots. Then we spray your carpet with our aggressive but safe cleaning agent. After soaking for several minutes, we use hot water extraction to remove the soil and cleaner.

Following the extraction, we treat any stains a second time that are still visible. This is the cleaning method required by carpet manufacturers to maintain your carpet warranty.

More

Furniture

We clean furniture according to IICRC and manufacturer recommendations. We can get your furniture as close to new as possible while protecting your investment.

Tile

Tile and grout are pretreated with a cleaning agent. Then we use scrubbing and high-pressure hot water extraction to restore your tile and grout to the best possible condition.

Hardwood

We use a three step deep cleaning and finish preservation process to maintain the appearance and original finish of your valuable hardwood floors.

Vinyl

Luxury vinyl plank flooring is currently a very popular choice. With proper professional cleaning it can last a lifetime. Our floor scrubber removes build up regular mopping leaves behind.
