About us

Barefoot Floor & Fabric Care is Matt and Melissa Cooper. We have one goal: to offer our customers the best value on the cleanest floors and furniture on the beach.

If you have carpet, furniture, tile, hardwood, or vinyl that need to be cleaned, please give us a call at 252-548-9030 and we'll stop by to give you a free estimate.

We serve all areas of the northern Outer Banks of North Carolina including but not limited to Corolla, Duck, Kitty Hawk, Kill Devil Hills, Nags Head, Manteo, Wanchese, Rodanthe, Waves, Avon, Hatteras, and Frisco.
